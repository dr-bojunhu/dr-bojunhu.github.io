Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I see you if I live outside of Texas?

    Yes. I am certified through PSYPACT and can work with clients in over 40 participating states.

  • How much do you charge?

    Fees vary depending on the type of sessions:
    Individual sessions: $210. Couples sessions: $315 for 90-minutes. Group sessions: $70-100 per 90-minutes.

  • Do you accept insurances?

    I am in-network with a limited number of insurance plans and primarily work on a private-pay basis. I'm happy to provide superbills for you to submit to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement. Please reach out to learn more about current options.
