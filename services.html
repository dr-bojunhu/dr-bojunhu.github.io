Services Overview

After an initial consultation to understand your history, current challenges, and hopes for therapy, we collaborate on a treatment process that aligns with your needs and values. Whether you choose individual therapy, couples work, or group therapy, this is where transformation unfolds—not by avoiding discomfort, but by discovering your capacity to meet experience with greater awareness and support. Together, we learn what your window of tolerance is and gently widen it, expanding your ability to feel, reflect, and relate.

Specialized Theraputic Services

  1. Individual Therapy: Individual therapy provides a dedicated space to explore your inner world with depth and care. Sessions focus on emotional experience, bodily awareness, and relational patterns as they emerge in the therapeutic relationship. Together, we attend to what feels stuck, fragmented, or overwhelming, while supporting new ways of experiencing yourself with greater integration and self-compassion.

  2. Group Therapy: Group therapy offers a powerful space to experience yourself in relationship with others in real time. Rather than focusing only on insight, groups emphasize lived, embodied interaction—how emotions arise, how the body responds, and how connection and disconnection are negotiated moment by moment. Over time, participants experience a growing sense of coherence, universality, and belonging, learning to stay present to themselves while engaging authentically with others.

  3. Couples Therapy: Couples therapy is a relational laboratory where patterns of connection, conflict, and longing can be seen more clearly. Through in-session experiences, partners learn to recognize emotional and bodily responses, practice new forms of communication, and develop greater capacity for mutual understanding, repair, and intimacy.
