Reaching out for support can feel like a leap into the unknown, especially when seeking a space to witness your most tender moments. My practice is dedicated to creating a safe, grounding environment where all parts of you are welcome. Together, we will hold space for curiosity about how you relate to the world and others, allowing what was once unsaid to become deeply felt and known.

Philosophy of Care

My approach is rooted in the belief that our physical, emotional, and cultural lives are inextricably linked. As a somatic relational therapist, I help you understand the cultural and relational backgrounds that may leave you feeling "stuck." By integrating mind-body awareness, we work to release what has stagnated in the body and cultivate new, authentic ways of being.

Whether you are navigating the complexities of trauma, transitioning into a new phase of life, or seeking deeper connection in your relationships, you do not have to do it alone.

Clinical Specialties

I provide individual, couples, and group psychotherapy, with a focus on:

  • Embodied Relational Therapy: Integrating body-awareness, yoga, and movement to enact new relational alternatives.
  • Trauma-Informed Care: Supporting survivors of trauma through somatic processing and emotional resilience.
  • Cultural & Life Transitions: Attending to the unique experiences of immigration, acculturation, and identity development.
  • Group Dynamics: Utilizing group settings to enhance collaborative dialogue and interpersonal learning.
