First Session Intention

The first session is an opportunity to begin forming a collaborative relationship. Rather than needing to know exactly what to say or where things will go, you are invited to arrive as you are and allow the work to unfold.

What to Expect in Your First Therapy Session

  • We will discuss what brings you to therapy, your background, and what feels most important right now.
  • We will review important logistics, including confidentiality and boundaries.
  • You are welcome to notice what arises—thoughts, emotions, bodily sensations—without pressure to perform or explain.

How Do I Make the Most of My Online Sessions?

  • Create a supportive physical space
  • Ensure a stable internet connection
  • Keep comforting or grounding objects nearby
  • Minimize interruptions and distractions
  • Allow a buffer before and after sessions to transition
